## Actions Taken

### New Skills Created

1. **`skills/admin/technician-candidate-phone-screen.md`** (v1.0) — Structured phone-screen workflow for plumber, service-tech, and apprentice candidates. Produces four sections: pre-call prep sheet with flags from the résumé, 3 knockout questions designed to end a bad-fit call in 90 seconds, role-calibrated 8–12 question interview script with good/okay/bad answer anchors and scenario probes tailored by role (service / drain / new-construction / apprentice), and a one-page post-call summary with hire/maybe/pass recommendation. Includes a fully worked example for a TX Journeyman candidate.

2. **`skills/operations/sewer-camera-inspection-report.md`** (v1.0) — Converts raw tech field notes from a CCTV sewer inspection into a customer-facing report with six sections: header/executive summary, inspection setup, defect table keyed to PACP-compatible observation codes, line-condition-by-segment triage (serviceable/degraded/failing), good/better/best repair recommendations with warranty and price bands, and an explicit "what the camera did not see" limitations block. Includes full example for a clay lateral on a pre-sale inspection where cable hit a hard stop at 52 ft.

3. **`skills/customer-service/pre-visit-diagnostic-intake.md`** (v1.0) — Front-office intake script (for dispatcher, AI receptionist, or chatbot) that does triage + pre-diagnosis + customer mitigation in one flow. Produces three sections: the conversational intake script with branches and safety micro-scripts, a dispatch-ready structured intake record with pre-diagnosis and truck-load implications, and an optional customer mitigation SMS/email. 4-tier urgency taxonomy (Emergency / Urgent / Standard / Scheduled) with plumbing-specific criteria. Full example for an after-hours water heater tank failure.

4. **`skills/customer-service/job-status-update-drafter.md`** (v1.0) — Stage-aware message drafter covering the full job lifecycle: booked, reminder, on-the-way, running-late, on-site, delay/parts-run, complete, and post-visit care. Produces stage-labeled SMS and email drafts with character/word counts, tech-by-name personalization, and a "when NOT to send" suppression note per stage. Closes the loop between Pre-Visit Diagnostic Intake and Review Request Drafter. Full example for a running-late scenario, with abbreviated drafts for complete, post-visit care, booked, reminder, and on-the-way stages.

## Industry Trends Noted

- **AI-driven trades hiring is the newest high-leverage workflow** — Phone-first screening (Classet/Joy) is a direct fit for plumbing because candidates are usually on the job, not at a desk. Expect more shops to adopt this pattern in 2026 as the labor shortage deepens.
- **CCTV sewer inspection is becoming table stakes, not differentiator** — Hardware cost drop (EasyCAM, Milwaukee M18) plus AI-assisted defect coding (SewerAI, WinCan) is turning every service shop into a potential inspection provider. The reporting quality — not the camera — is now the competitive edge.
- **AI receptionist platforms are converging on the same intake pattern** — AgentZap, Voiceflow, My AI Front Desk, Avoca all collect roughly the same 8 fields. The script matters more than the vendor; shops running a human dispatcher can benefit from the same structured intake without adopting a voice-AI product.
- **Customer communication lifecycle is finally being automated end-to-end** — Shops that send only one or two status updates per job are losing to shops that send six. Post-visit care messages are the sleeper differentiator.
- **OSHA / compliance / warranty trends from prior cycles remain stable** — No new developments worth action this cycle.
- **Still no plumbing-specific AI prompt repos on GitHub** — Third consecutive run with the same result. KRASA repos remain the only known open-source plumbing AI skills collection.
